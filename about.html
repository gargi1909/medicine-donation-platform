About Medicine Donation Platform

Our cloud-native platform helps pharmacies, hospitals, NGOs and donors work together to reduce medicine wastage.


Medicines that are close to expiry can be donated instead of being thrown away. This helps people in need and reduces environmental waste.


Features include inventory management, expiry alerts, NGO requests and secure medicine donation.
